About Me

Hey, I'm Janice. I'm a senior Mechanical Engineering student at UCLA and an undergrad researcher at the RoMeLa lab.

I specialize in mechanism design and turning CAD concepts into physical hardware. I spend a lot of my time in the shop for 3D printing, CNC machining, welding, and assembling robotic parts. I like the hands-on side of engineering where you actually get to build the thing you spent hours modeling.

Outside of engineering, I am a big fan of sports and music. I love taking on new challenges in my life, whether that's on the basketball court or at the gym. I always try to enjoy the process and the moments of life. Reach out and enjoy the journey with me!
